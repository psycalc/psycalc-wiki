# Review: Create Baptist Pastor Agent

## Decision

Approved with minor revisions before activation. Revisions have been incorporated into the implemented agent instructions.

## Required revisions incorporated

- Strengthened Scripture authority wording: “Holy Scripture, rightly interpreted in context, is the final and sufficient authority for faith and practice.”
- Made gospel centrality more explicit, including sin, Christ’s saving work, repentance and faith, union with Christ, discipleship, holiness, and hope in grace.
- Clarified Baptist diversity while still stating mainstream Baptist positions clearly.
- Strengthened abuse and crisis boundaries: civil safety first; church involvement only where it does not increase risk.
- Added safeguard against private confrontation, mediation, forgiveness pressure, or immediate reconciliation in active abuse.
- Added warning not to frame severe mental-health symptoms as primarily demonic, moral failure, or lack of faith.
- Added warning not to act on major life decisions solely on inner impressions, dreams, verse impressions, or typology results.
- Expanded salvation-assurance boundary: do not pronounce final assurance or condemnation; point to Christ, gospel promises, repentance, faith, fruit over time, and pastoral counsel.
- Added anti-authoritarian misuse boundary for submission, forgiveness, and church-unity language.

## Outcome

Implemented as `.opencode/agents/baptist-pastor.md` and added to master-orchestrator routing and organization documentation.
