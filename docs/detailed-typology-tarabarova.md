### Detailed Breakdown

#### Position 1: Ne (External Intuition) — Leading

**What it means**: She's driven by creative vision, seeing possibilities, generating new ideas.

**Evidence**:
- Started 2008 on "Fabrika zirok", then created own solo style in 2012
- Creates complete albums as "musical films" (e.g., "23:25" 2018)

**How it manifests**:
- "I see the song before it exists"
- Creates innovative formats (albums as movies)
- Always new creative directions

#### Position 2: Fi (Internal Ethics) — Creative

**What it means**: She develops her emotional expression in depth, authentic personal values.

**Evidence**:
- Songs are emotionally deep ("Вірю.Знаю", "Воїн")
- Creates songs about personal experiences
- "Warrior" song with Yarmak about military — emotional depth over commercial
- Family values: 3 children, married 2016

**How it manifests**:
- Music from personal truth, not fabricated emotions
- Emotional authenticity in performances
- Values connection over popularity

#### Position 3: Te (External Logic) — Role

**What it means**: She presents structured, organized approach to the outside world when needed.

**Evidence**:
- Runs production business, manages career
- Creates complete album structures (not random songs)
- Reality show "Декрет вже не секрет" — organized content
- National selections for Eurovision production

**How it manifests**:
- Professional organization of creative work
- Business aspect of music career
- Structured albums, scheduled releases

#### Position 4: Si (Internal Sensing) — Vulnerable

**What it means**: Physical/sensory comfort is her sensitive point, may neglect own physical needs.

**Evidence**:
- Stage performer (physically demanding)
- 3 children in 5 years (2018, 2020, 2023) — intensive body use
- Creates content about pregnancy experiences

**How it manifests**:
- May overwork physically
- Body/sensory comfort not priority
- Tendency to prioritize performance over rest
