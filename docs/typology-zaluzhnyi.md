### Aspect: Present-Future (PF)

**Rationale**:
- Works intensely in current reality of war
- Plans for long-term victory ("entire territory must be liberated")
- Urgency combined with strategic patience

### Temporal Profile

| Ring | Aspect | Evidence |
|------|--------|----------|
| **1st** | Present: Current battlefield, daily responsibilities | "Every day, it's those who were killed. Every day, it's the maimed, the missing." |
| **2nd** | Past: 2014-2022 experience | War in Donbas "shaped his idea of creating this flexible structure" |
| **3rd** | Future: Victory, NATO integration | "Our victory is, unequivocally, the liberation of the entire occupied territory" |
| **4th** | Eternity: Legacy, freedom | "Transformation of Ukrainian armed forces... irreversible" |

**Type**: PF (Present-Future orientation)

**Interpretation**: Zaluzhnyi operates primarily in present reality while building toward future goals. He's not dwelling on past (Soviet legacy) but actively transforming it into future (NATO standards).

| Function | SLI (gamma) | Evidence |
