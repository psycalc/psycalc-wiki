## Key Findings

### Global (MBTI)
1. **SJ types dominate** — 46% of population (ISFJ, ESFJ, ISTJ, ESTJ)
2. **Intuitive types rare** — Only 26.7% vs 73.3% Sensing
3. **INFJ rarest overall** — 1.5% globally
4. **ISFJ most common** — 13.8% globally
5. **Gender patterns clear** — Women more Feeling, Men more Thinking

### Russia (Socionics)
1. **Intuitive types more common** — Contrasts with global Sensing dominance
2. **Alphaquadra (IE, LN)** underrepresented
3. **Beta/Gamma dominate** — 59% combined
4. **Dostoevsky rarest** — Only 1% in Russia (vs 7.7% global NF average)

## Caveats

1. **Self-typing bias** — Most online stats come from self-reported tests
2. **Cultural differences** — Russia has more Intuitive types than USA
3. **Sampling bias** — People interested in typology may differ from general population
4. **16 Personalities vs MBTI** — Different systems, slightly different distributions
5. **Terra Socionika** — Sample is mostly Moscow residents with higher education
