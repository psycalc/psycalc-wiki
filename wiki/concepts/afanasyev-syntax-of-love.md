# Syntax of Love

## Overview

"Syntax of Love" (Синтаксис Любви) is the foundational book by Alexander Afanasyev that introduced the Psychosophy (PsychoYoga) personality model.

## Key Concepts

- Four aspects: Physics, Logic, Will, Emotion
- Function expression levels (1st through 4th)
- Intertype relationship dynamics
- Greek models (Eros, Agape, Philia, Storge)
