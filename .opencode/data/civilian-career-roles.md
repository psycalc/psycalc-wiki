## Role Families

### DevOps / SRE / Platform Engineering

- Tags: `tech-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `analysis-heavy`, `stress-heavy`, `long-horizon`, `coordination-heavy`
- Typical tasks: infrastructure automation, CI/CD, monitoring, incident response, reliability engineering
- Good signals: systems thinking, practical diagnostics, tolerance for operational responsibility
- Risks: alert fatigue, unclear ownership, chaotic on-call culture

### Systems / Network / Cloud Administration

- Tags: `tech-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `hands-on`, `analysis-heavy`, `routine-heavy`
- Typical tasks: servers, networks, access, backups, cloud resources, troubleshooting
- Good signals: maintenance mindset, precision, patience with infrastructure
- Risks: repetitive ticket work, legacy systems, after-hours incidents

### Cybersecurity Operations

- Tags: `tech-heavy`, `analysis-heavy`, `stress-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `long-horizon`
- Typical tasks: monitoring, incident triage, vulnerability management, access control, forensics support
- Good signals: calm under pressure, pattern detection, disciplined process
- Risks: shift work, alert overload, adversarial pressure

### Data / BI / Operations Analysis

- Tags: `analysis-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `tech-heavy`, `coordination-heavy`
- Typical tasks: dashboards, reporting, data cleanup, process analysis, decision support
- Good signals: explanation, metrics, structured thinking
- Risks: stakeholder ambiguity, low data quality, repetitive reporting

### QA / Test Automation

- Tags: `tech-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `analysis-heavy`, `routine-heavy`
- Typical tasks: test plans, automation, regression checks, release quality, bug triage
- Good signals: consistency, defect detection, practical logic
- Risks: low autonomy, undervalued gatekeeper role, monotonous cycles

### Technical Writing / Documentation

- Tags: `structure-heavy`, `analysis-heavy`, `coordination-heavy`, `creative-heavy`
- Typical tasks: docs, runbooks, knowledge bases, API documentation, onboarding guides
- Good signals: explanatory logic, clarity, continuity preservation
- Risks: low feedback, dependence on SMEs, less hands-on technical work

### Technical Project / Release / Operations Management

- Tags: `coordination-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `stress-heavy`, `people-heavy`, `long-horizon`
- Typical tasks: releases, planning, stakeholder coordination, risk tracking, process control
- Good signals: structured communication, planning, cross-team translation
- Risks: politics, status games, meeting overload, accountability without authority

### Implementation / Solutions Engineering

- Tags: `tech-heavy`, `people-heavy`, `coordination-heavy`, `analysis-heavy`
- Typical tasks: deploy customer solutions, troubleshoot integrations, translate needs into technical setup
- Good signals: technical explanation, practical adaptation, bounded client contact
- Risks: sales pressure, emotional labor, unclear scope

### Technical Support / Customer Success Engineering

- Tags: `people-heavy`, `tech-heavy`, `stress-heavy`, `care-heavy`, `coordination-heavy`
- Typical tasks: complex support, escalation handling, customer education, incident communication
- Good signals: patience, explanation, technical empathy
- Risks: emotional pressure, angry users, low control over product issues

### Training / Instruction / Mentoring

- Tags: `people-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `creative-heavy`, `care-heavy`
- Typical tasks: teach skills, prepare materials, mentor juniors, run workshops
- Good signals: explanatory ability, emotional tone-setting, patience
- Risks: audience management, repetitive basics, unstable demand

### Logistics / Operations Coordination

- Tags: `coordination-heavy`, `structure-heavy`, `hands-on`, `stress-heavy`, `routine-heavy`
- Typical tasks: schedules, inventory, dispatch, process control, vendor coordination
- Good signals: practical organization, continuity, reliability
- Risks: time pressure, many interruptions, low strategic autonomy

### Field / Hardware / Industrial Technician

- Tags: `hands-on`, `field-heavy`, `tech-heavy`, `stress-heavy`, `routine-heavy`
- Typical tasks: equipment installation, repair, maintenance, inspection, site operations
- Good signals: practical sensing, real-world troubleshooting, comfort with material systems
- Risks: travel, physical load, weather/site constraints

### Product / Business Analysis

- Tags: `analysis-heavy`, `people-heavy`, `coordination-heavy`, `long-horizon`, `structure-heavy`
- Typical tasks: requirements, user stories, process mapping, stakeholder interviews, acceptance criteria
- Good signals: translation between user needs and technical work
- Risks: ambiguity, politics, constant reprioritization

### Sales / Business Development

- Tags: `sales-heavy`, `people-heavy`, `stress-heavy`, `autonomy-high`
- Typical tasks: prospecting, persuasion, negotiation, pipeline management
- Good signals: drive, social assertiveness, status tolerance
- Risks: rejection, pressure quotas, performative confidence

### Care / Psychology / Social Support

- Tags: `care-heavy`, `people-heavy`, `stress-heavy`, `long-horizon`
- Typical tasks: support people, counseling-adjacent work, case coordination, crisis response
- Good signals: emotional steadiness, ethical attention, patience
- Risks: compassion fatigue, boundary load, institutional pressure

## Notes

- Prefer role families over exact job titles.
- Match against real constraints: salary, country, language, education, remote/on-site, health, and market demand.
- Typology is a hypothesis generator. Proven performance and observed preferences are stronger evidence.
